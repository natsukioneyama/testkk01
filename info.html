KAZUKO KITAOKA HAIR STYLIST

BIOGRAPHY

Born and raised in Osaka, Japan, Kazuko Kitaoka moved to Paris in 2009.

She works as a hair stylist, focusing on fashion editorials and advertising campaigns. Specializing in natural hairstyling without the use of wigs, she creates looks that bring out each individual’s natural beauty.

Through her editorial work, she has also developed a strong interest in celebrity hairstyling, further expanding the scope of her creative expression.
